Frequently Asked Questions

Can I update my password?
Yes, you can. You have to go to the profile page and click update password.
If you're having problems login in, you can always reset your password via email by clicking Forgot Password
Can I change my avatar?
Sure, you can! All you have to do is to visit your profile page, and click Edit Profile button.
There, you have to select a file and then click Update.
Voilaaa!
